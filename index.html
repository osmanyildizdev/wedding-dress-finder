How it works

A beautiful bridal experience starts with you.

Height, bust, waist, hips, venue, budget, favorite silhouettes, fabric feeling, and wedding date all shape the recommendation. The result is a guided shortlist instead of an overwhelming wall of dresses.

01 Measure

Enter your basic measurements.

Start with height, bust, waist, hips, and heel height. Final fitting measurements are always refined in the atelier.

02 Choose

Tell us what feels like you.

Pick your venue, budget, favorite silhouettes, and whether you feel romantic, minimal, glamorous, playful, or bohemian.

03 Match

See your best dress candidates.

Your top matches appear with style notes, fit logic, and a clear path to booking a warm, relaxed fitting.

Fitting experience

A fitting should feel like being understood, not being sold to.

We begin with your story: how you want to walk, dance, hug, breathe, and look in photographs. Then we shape the dress around your body, your budget, and your wedding atmosphere.

Body proportion reading

We look at waist placement, shoulder balance, length, and movement before narrowing down silhouettes.

Fabric feeling

Try tulle, lace, satin, organza, mikado, and pearl accents under warm showroom light.

Complete styling

Veil, gloves, hair accessories, shoes, and bouquet mood are considered as one finished bridal look.
