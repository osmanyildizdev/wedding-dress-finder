Atelier

A warm fitting room for modern brides with real budgets.

Luma Bridal is imagined as a boutique where affordability never means cold, rushed, or generic. The experience is gentle, colorful, and personal from the first measurement to the final veil.

First visit

Try silhouettes, not pressure.

Your stylist listens to your venue, budget, comfort needs, and dream photos before choosing gowns.

Second fitting

Refine the dress around you.

Hem, waist, bust support, sleeve options, and movement are adjusted with care.

Final styling

Finish the whole bridal look.

Veil, accessories, shoes, hair direction, and dress care are prepared for the wedding day.

What we believe

Every bride deserves beauty without feeling priced out of the room.

The Luma promise is simple: warm guidance, transparent price ranges, flattering silhouettes, and enough styling imagination to make a modern affordable dress feel deeply personal.

Transparent pricing
