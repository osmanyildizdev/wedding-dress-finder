Stylist note

The form is here to guide you, not box you in.

Many brides fall in love with a silhouette they did not expect. That is why Luma uses your answers as a warm starting point, then confirms everything through movement, posture, fabric, and feeling.

If you want comfort

Prioritize lighter skirts, softer linings, and silhouettes that let you sit, hug, and dance without thinking about the dress.

If you want drama

Choose volume, trains, pearl shine, and structured bodices that photograph beautifully from every angle.

If you want value

Start with a clean base dress and add veil, sleeves, detachable overskirt, or accessories to create more than one look.
